Support

Frequently asked questions

Our AI vision model analyses any fashion item — from a photo, link or text description — breaking it down into core attributes like fabric, cut, colour, silhouette and construction. It then scores thousands of products from Zara, H&M, Mango, ASOS, COS and & Other Stories against those attributes in real time, surfacing the closest matches ranked by accuracy.

Our Story

Fashion should be for everyone

ALTERE was born from a simple frustration: falling in love with a runway piece, then seeing the price tag. We believe great style shouldn't require a trust fund.

So we built an AI that sees fashion the way a stylist does — analysing fabric, cut, colour and silhouette — then scours thousands of high-street products to find the closest match at a fraction of the cost.

The Technology

How our AI works

01

Visual deconstruction

Our vision model breaks down any fashion item into its core attributes: material, texture, shape, proportions, colour palette and construction details.

02

Cross-store matching

We scan inventories from Zara, H&M, Mango, ASOS, COS and & Other Stories in real time, scoring each product against the original on over 40 style dimensions.

03

Smart ranking

Results are ranked by match accuracy, price savings and availability — so the best dupe always rises to the top.
